How to add numbers

Below you will find instructions for adding numbers using the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully add numbers, you must know what addition is. Refer to What is Addition? to learn about addition.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equals (=) button.

The result from the addition is displayed.

Example:

You want to add 2 to 3. The equation is 3 + 2 = 5.

Enter 3 Press plus Enter 2 Press equals Result '5' displays
