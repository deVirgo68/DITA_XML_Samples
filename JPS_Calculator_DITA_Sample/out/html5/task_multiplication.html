How to multiply numbers

Below you will find instructions for multiplying 2 numbers using the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully add numbers, you must know what multiplication is.Refer to What is Multiplication? to learn about multiplication.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the multiply (x) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equals (=) button.

The result (product) from the multiplication is displayed.

Example:

You want to multiply 3 and 2. The equation is 3 x 2 = 6.

Enter 3 Press multiply Enter 2 Press equals Result '6' displays
