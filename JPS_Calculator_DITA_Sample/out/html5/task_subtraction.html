How to subtract numbers

Below you will find instructions for subtracting numbers using JPS Calculator app.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what subtraction is. Refer to What is Subtraction? to learn about subtraction.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the minus (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equals (=) button.

The result from the subtraction is displayed.

Example:

You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation is 3 - 2 = 1.

Enter 3 Press minus Enter 2 Press equals Result '1' displays
